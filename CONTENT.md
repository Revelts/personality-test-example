## Overview
Tes kepribadian ini dirancang dengan pendekatan **product-minded psychology** - menggabungkan validitas psikologis dengan user experience yang engaging dan fun.

- **Bahasa**: 100% Bahasa Indonesia
- **Tone**: Casual, friendly, relatable
- **Style**: Bukan formal/akademis, tapi tetap insightful
- **Target**: Gen Z dan Millennials Indonesia

## Sistem Scoring

### Mekanisme
- Setiap jawaban memberikan +1 point ke satu dimensi dominan
- Total 20 pertanyaan = maksimal 20 point tersebar di 5 dimensi
- Hasil final based on kombinasi 2 dimensi tertinggi

### Contoh Perhitungan
```
User A scores:
- Logical: 8
- Creative: 6
- Empathetic: 3
- Leader: 2
- Adventurer: 1

Primary: Logical (8)
Secondary: Creative (6)
Result: Si Visionary (Logical + Creative)
```

## Micro-Interactions (Jokes)

### Purpose
- Keep engagement tinggi
- Prevent survey fatigue
- Add personality ke experience
- Context-aware humor

### Style
- **Relatable**: Situasi yang people can relate
- **Light**: Bukan offensive, inklusif
- **Validating**: Make user feel understood
- **Playful**: Pake emoji, casual language

## Hasil Tes (Result Card)

### Komponen
1. **Title**: Nama tipe kepribadian (e.g., "Si Mastermind")
2. **Tagline**: Catchy phrase yang capture essence
3. **Description**: 2-3 kalimat describe personality type
4. **Traits**: 4 karakteristik utama
5. **Strengths**: 4 kekuatan dominan
6. **Emoji**: Visual representation
7. **Color**: Gradient yang match personality

### Tone pada Result
- **Empowering**: Focus on strengths
- **Validating**: Make them feel understood
- **Positive**: Even "weakness" dijelaskan as characteristic
- **Actionable**: Bisa applied to real life
